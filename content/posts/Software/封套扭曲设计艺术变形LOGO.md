> [!info]- 封套扭曲的本质
> 封套扭曲是变形了的剪切蒙版，可以根据上层形状，扭曲文字
> 
## 1. 方法
1. 分割图形
	- 填补空缺![](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511241159918.png)
	- 分割图形尽量圆润![](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511241202023.png)![](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511241208091.png)
		- 分割如果不够圆润，封套扭曲后就会显示不清楚 ![](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511241222632.png)
2. 封套扭曲
	- 文字放在图形下面![](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511241226564.png)
	- 选中图形和文字，`左键+C` ( `alt ctrl c` )
3. 进一步调整
	-  调整字间距![](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511241234413.png)
	- 扩展外观后拉动最后一个字母，做出尾巴效果![](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511241239000.png)
