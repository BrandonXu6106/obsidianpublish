## 1. 风格分析步骤
1. 搜集参考
2. 分析共同要点
	- 构图特征
	- 形式特征
	- 色彩特征
## 2. 极简风
- **构图特征**
	- 多为==上中下==构图/==绕排==构图![202511051732724.png|461](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511051732724.png)
	- 中间区域放置图像![202511051733011.png|686](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511051733011.png)
	- 文本排布可以看到使用网格的痕迹或思路 ![202511051733011.png|547](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511051733011.png)
- **形式特征**
	- 文案内容通常较为==简洁==，俏皮
	  ![202511052100202.png](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511052100202.png)
	  ![202511052100340.png|480](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511052100340.png)
	- 字体常为==黑体==搭配==无衬线==体![510](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511051730655.png)
	- 字体==对比==明显，==分组==明确![202511051731888.png|740](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511051731888.png)![202511051730619.png|783](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511051730619.png)
	- 图像展示形式
		- 产品为==抠图==
		- 白底或其他==纯色底==图文之间有前后穿插![202511051726626.png|457](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511051726626.png)
		- 偶尔增加其他的==图形==样式进行额外==点缀==![202511052108152.png|478](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511052108152.png)
		- 可以先展示产品局部，再展示整体![202511061905011.png|265](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511061905011.png)
- **色彩特征**
	- 白底或其他==单色==底
	- 底色多为==低饱和度==的色彩区间![467](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511052113273.png)
	- 和==产品色调==相符![202511052113169.png|514](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511052113169.png)![202511052114341.png|857](https://int-1330323257.cos.ap-guangzhou.myqcloud.com//obsidian/pic/202511052114341.png)
