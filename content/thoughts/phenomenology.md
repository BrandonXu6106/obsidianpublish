In phenomenology, the source of all meaning and value is through the subjective lived experience of conscious beings.

This is the argument _against_ independent origination or svabhāva, the very thing [Buddhist](Buddhism.md) texts claim that _everything_ we perceive is [empty](emptiness.md) of.
