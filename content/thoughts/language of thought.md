Can we use [[language.md|language]] as the method of querying memory?

## Language of thought hypothesis

Proposes thinking occurs in a mental language called mentalese.

[[computability.md|Computations]] occur in a classical way (over symbols in mentalese). However, [[neural networks|neural networks](neural%20networks.md) or [connectionist networks](connectionist%20networks.md) challenges any argument for mentalese (as they don't fall under the [[GOFAI.md|GOFAI]]/classical computation category)
