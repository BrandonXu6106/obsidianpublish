If there is no profile where one player’s payoff is better but no player’s payoff is worse. Pareto optimality is a criterion of ‘group [[rationality.md]]’, not a criterion for individual rationality (see: [[Nash equilibrium](Nash%20equilibrium.md))

Usually only applicable for [[positive sum](positive%20sum.md) games
