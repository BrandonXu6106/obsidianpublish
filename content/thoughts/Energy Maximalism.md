## Regulation
The Nuclear Regulatory Commision (NRC) found that there is no evidence of a carcinogenic effect on humans for accute irradiation at doses less than 100 millisieverts (mSv). Yet, the same NARC also sets the limit for public radiation at just 1% of that number at 1mSv. This is like setting a speed limit of one mile per hour because people have died doing 100. Yes, [[Nuclear Fusion|nuclear](Nuclear%20Fusion.md) today is expensive. Shipping would be, too, if trucks had to operate with a speed limit of one mile per hour.
